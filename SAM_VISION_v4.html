Data Sources & Methodology
All prices are Q1 2026 estimates from authoritative Indian sources.
📊 Primary Price Sources
  • MagicBricks & 99acres Insight Reports — Locality-level ₹/sqft, QoQ & YoY trends (Jan 2026)
  • Knight Frank India Real Estate Report 2025 — Premium & luxury segment pricing, city-wide indices
  • Anarock Research Q4 2025 — Residential launch prices, unsold inventory, absorption rates
  • CREDAI Housing Outlook 2026 — Affordable housing prices, RERA-registered project data
  • NoBroker Market Pulse — Rental yield data, actual transaction vs listed prices
🏛️ Government & Regulatory Sources
  • RERA Portal (state-wise) — Registered project prices, developer data
  • RBI Housing Price Index (HPI) — Macro price movement by city tier
  • IGR Maharashtra / State Stamp Duty portals — Actual registered transaction values
  • PMAY & Affordable Housing Scheme data — Sub-₹6,000/sqft market benchmarks
📐 Valuation Methodology
  • Base Rate: Median ₹/sqft from 3+ sources per locality, weighted by transaction volume
  • Segment Multiplier: Anarock affordability matrix — 0.55× (affordable) to 2.2× (ultra-luxury)
  • Growth CAGR: 5-year historical + 2-year forward from Knight Frank city outlook
  • Rental Yield: NoBroker monthly rent, annualised per RBI yield framework
  • EMI: SBI home loan rate 8.5% p.a., per RBI lending norms
⚠️ Disclaimer

For informational and academic use only. Prices are indicative Q1 2026 estimates. Always verify with RERA, local registrar offices, and a certified property valuer before transacting.
